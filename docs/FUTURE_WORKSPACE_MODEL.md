# Future Workspace parent

`Account` remains one marketplace-specific seller account and is the authorization boundary for imports, scans, work queues, mutations, and live updates.

A future business umbrella such as “Sujal Mumbai” may be represented by an additive `Workspace` parent with several `Account` children. It must not weaken selected-account authorization or cause worker queries to search sibling accounts. No Workspace model is implemented in Phase 7.3.
