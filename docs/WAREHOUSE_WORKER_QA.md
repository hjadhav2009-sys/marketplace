Use only fake or sanitized work.

1. Import and activate one Flipkart consignment.
2. Import and activate one Amazon consignment with listing/catalog enrichment.
3. Correct one missing or ambiguous listing match.
4. Pick across two assigned accounts.
5. Open Mark details and verify identity, instructions, settings, and images.
6. Complete Pack and verify line/batch completion.
7. Pick an assembly-required customer order, complete Assembly, then Pack.
8. Have two workers contend for one task; verify one assignment and a clear message.
9. Report a problem, then owner resolve/reassign and resume.
10. Scan quickly through several codes; verify lookup never mutates and completed work is non-actionable.

Record device, browser, network path, observed latency, and sanitized issue notes. Never commit screenshots or warehouse data.
